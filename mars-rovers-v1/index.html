The Mars Exploration Project

Mars has long captured our imagination, and today we’re closer than ever to understanding its secrets. NASA’s Curiosity and Perseverance rovers are exploring the Red Planet with cutting-edge tools, searching for clues about ancient water, past environments, and the possibility of life. Their discoveries are helping us piece together the story of a world both familiar and alien.
